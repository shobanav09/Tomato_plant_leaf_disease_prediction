General Info


Target spot of tomato is a serious foliar disease that affects both greenhouse and field-grown tomatoes.

Pathogens


Target spot of tomato is caused by the fungal pathogen Corynespora cassiicola

Symptoms & Signs


  • Circular spots with concentric, target-like rings
  • A velvety black, fungal lesion in the center
  • The spots becoming pitted and larger as the tomato matures
  • Some spots enlarging up to 10 mm and showing characteristic rings
